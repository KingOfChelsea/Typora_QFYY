# 📚 雅思高频词汇/短语与例句(day1)

#### 1. **Conducive**

- **释义**：有利于…的
- **例句**：A quiet environment is **conducive** to focused study.
- **一词多义**：仅作形容词，表示"创造有利条件的"[4](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 2. **Detrimental**

- **释义**：有害的
- **例句**：Smoking is **detrimental** to lung health.
- **同义词**：deleterious, hazardous[4](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 3. **Address**

- **释义1**（动词）：处理
- **例句**：The government must **address** climate change urgently.
- **释义2**（名词）：演讲；地址
- **例句**：The manager gave an **address** at the annual conference[6](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 4. **Account for**

- **释义1**：解释；占据
- **例句**：Can you **account for** the missing data?
- **释义2**：在统计中表示比例
- **例句**：Renewable energy **accounts for** 20% of total usage[6](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 5. **Fuel**

- **释义1**（动词）：刺激，助长
- **例句**：Social media can **fuel** unrealistic expectations.
- **释义2**（名词）：燃料
- **例句**：The demand for clean **fuel** is rising[4](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 6. **Appreciate**

- **释义1**：理解；体会
- **例句**：I **appreciate** the challenges faced by immigrants.
- **释义2**：欣赏；升值
- **例句**：We **appreciate** your contribution to the project[7](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 7. **Subject**

- **释义1**（名词）：主题；实验对象
- **例句**：The **subject** of the lecture is artificial intelligence.
- **释义2**（形容词）：易受…影响的
- **例句**：Coastal cities are **subject** to flooding[7](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 8. **Compound**

- **释义1**（动词）：加剧
- **例句**：Stress can **compound** existing health issues.
- **释义2**（名词）：化合物；院落
- **例句**：The chemical **compound** is unstable[5](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 9. **Project**

- **释义1**（动词）：预测；投射
- **例句**：The company **projects** a 10% growth next year.
- **释义2**（名词）：项目
- **例句**：The community **project** benefits local residents[6](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 10. **Issue**

- **释义1**（名词）：问题；期刊
- **例句**：Pollution is a critical **issue** in urban areas.
- **释义2**（动词）：发布
- **例句**：The agency **issued** a new guideline[6](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 11. **Due to**

- **释义**：由于（正式用语）
- **例句**：The flight delay was **due to** bad weather[3](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 12. **Pave the way for**

- **释义**：为…铺平道路
- **例句**：Technology **paves the way for** educational innovation[4](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 13. **As a result**

- **释义**：因此
- **例句**：He practiced daily; **as a result**, he won the competition[9](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 14. **In spite of**

- **释义**：尽管
- **例句**：**In spite of** the rain, the event continued[9](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 15. **Demanding**

- **释义**：要求高的；费力的
- **例句**：The **demanding** job requires overtime work[1,7](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 16. **Above board**

- **释义**：光明正大的
- **例句**：Ensure all transactions are **above board**[2](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 17. **Hold someone accountable**

- **释义**：要求某人负责
- **例句**：Managers are **held accountable** for team performance[4](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 18. **Bear in mind**

- **释义**：记住
- **例句**：**Bear in mind** that time is limited[10](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 19. **In the long run**

- **释义**：从长远看
- **例句**：Investing in education pays off **in the long run**[10](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 20. **Rather than**

- **释义**：而非
- **例句**：Walk **rather than** drive for short distances[10](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 21. **Break down**

- **释义1**：分解；抛锚
- **例句**：**<u>Enzymes(微生物)</u>** **break down** food in digestion.
- **释义2**：情绪崩溃
- **例句**：She **broke down** after the stressful event[5](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 22. **Yield**

- **释义1**（动词）：产生；屈服
- **例句**：The experiment **yielded** surprising results.
- **释义2**（名词）：产量
- **例句**：The farm’s **yield** increased by 15%.

#### 23. **Substitute**

- **释义**（动词/名词）：替代
- **例句**：Honey can **substitute** sugar in some recipes.

#### 24. **Given that**

- **释义**：鉴于
- **例句**：**Given that** time is limited, we must act quickly.

#### 25. **In terms of**

- **释义**：在…方面
- **例句**：The country is advanced **in terms of** technology.

#### 26. **Lead to**

- **释义**：导致
- **例句**：Poor communication can **lead to** misunderstandings[4](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 27. **On behalf of**

- **释义**：代表
- **例句**：She spoke **on behalf of** the entire team[11](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 28. **With regard to**

- **释义**：关于
- **例句**：**With regard to** your proposal, we need more details[11](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

#### 29. **Hospitable**

- **释义1**：热情好客的
- **例句**：The locals are **hospitable** to tourists.
- **释义2**：（环境）适宜的
- **例句**：The climate is **hospitable** for agriculture.

#### 30. **Make sense**

- **释义**：合理；讲得通
- **例句**：His explanation doesn’t **make sense**.

### 📖 综合应用短文

以下短文整合了上述词汇，帮助你理解其实际用法：

**主题：城市化与可持续发展**
 Rapid urbanization has ​**compounded**​ issues like pollution and resource scarcity. ​**Given that**​ cities consume 70% of global energy, policymakers must ​**address**​ these challenges by promoting renewable resources, which currently ​**account for**​ only 20% of usage. Strict regulations ​**pave the way for**​ cleaner industries, though implementation is ​**demanding**.

**In spite of** costs, green technology is **conducive** to long-term growth. For example, solar energy can **substitute** fossil fuels and **yield** economic benefits. **In the long run**, such efforts **make sense** as they reduce health risks **detrimental** to communities. Citizens and leaders should be **held accountable** to ensure policies are **above board**.

**With regard to** social impacts, inclusive planning is **hospitable** to all residents. **As a result**, cities can become resilient **in terms of** both ecology and equity[4,8,9](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。

### 💎 学习建议

1. **掌握一词多义**：如"address"和"issue"等词义随语境变化，需结合例句记忆[6,7](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。
2. **分类记忆**：将词汇分为"因果"（due to）、"对比"（rather than）、"影响"（conducive）等逻辑组块[9,10](https://tencent.yuanbao/@ref)。
3. **主动输出**：在写作中刻意使用这些词汇，并自查是否贴合语境。
