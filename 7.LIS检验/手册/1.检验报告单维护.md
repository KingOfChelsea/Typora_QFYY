## 1.2 查出后找到对应的条码号

- `NIS`用户下方便查询

```sql
select * from V_SAMPLE_INFO_NIS a where a.BARCODE = ''
```

- `WINLIS`用户下查询

```sql
SELECT 
    t.PATIENTNAME || ' (' || t.PATIENTSEX || '/' || t.PATIENTAGE || ')' AS 患者,
    t.BARCODE AS 条码,
    t.DOCTORNAME || ' ' || t.DEPTNAME AS 开立医生科室,
    t.SAMPLETYPE AS 样本类型,
    a.MACHINENAME AS 仪器,
    TO_CHAR(t.ORDERTIME, 'HH24:MI') || '→' || 
    TO_CHAR(t.RESULTTIME, 'HH24:MI') AS 开立_结果时间,
    CASE t.CONFIRMSTATE WHEN '1' THEN '已审核' ELSE '未审核' END AS 状态
FROM las_sap_samplereg t
LEFT JOIN las_com_machine a ON t.machineid = a.machineid
WHERE t.barcode = '2000456014'; -- 输入条形码
```
